PROMs dataset - casemix adjustment

Aggregating patient level scores to the provider level score

$$Adj Q2_j = Q2_{National Average} + \frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N(AQ2_{j, i} - PQ2_{j, i})$$

Where: AQ2j, i is the actual post-operative (Q2 score) for patient i treated at provider j PQ2j, i is the predicted post-operative (Q2 score) for patient i treated at provider j
